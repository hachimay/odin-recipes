Perfection Salad

A stunning crowd pleaser

Need we say more? Perfection Salad speaks for itself.

Ingredients

Makes 8 servings

  • 2 envelopes unflavored gelatine
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup finely shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1 can (4 oz) chopped pimento
  1. In a small saucepan, combine gelatine, sugar, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Add 1 cup water. Heat over low heat, stirring constantly until sugar and gelatine are dissolved. Remove from heat.
  3. Stir in apple juice, lemon juice, vinegar, and 1/4 cup cold water. Pour into medium bowl and refrigerate 1 hour, or until mixture is consistency of unbeaten egg whites.
  4. Add carrot, celery, cabbage, green pepper, and pimento. Stir until combined.
  5. Turn into decorative, 1 1/2 quart mold. Refrigerate 4 hours or until firm.
  6. To unmold: Run a small spatula around the edge of the mold, then invert onto your serving plate. Place a hot dishclth over the mold, and shake gently to release. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
